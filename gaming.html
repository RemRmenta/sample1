Essential Equipment

Whether highlighting intense action, deep storytelling, or multiplayer experiences, VR game trailers build anticipation and give players a glimpse of what to expect in the evolving world of virtual reality gaming.

Headset

Meta Quest Pro

A premium standalone VR headset by Meta, designed for both gaming and productivity. It features pancake lenses for a slimmer profile, full-color passthrough for mixed reality experiences, and eye and face tracking for enhanced social interactions.

Valve Index

A high-end PC VR headset known for its 120Hz refresh rate (with experimental 144Hz support), wide field of view (FOV), and precise finger-tracking controllers. It offers SteamVR tracking, making it one of the most accurate VR systems available, but requires external base stations for full motion tracking.

PlayStation VR2

Sony’s next-gen VR headset for PlayStation 5, offering 4K HDR resolution, eye-tracking for foveated rendering, adaptive triggers, and haptic feedback for deep immersion. Unlike the original PSVR, it features inside-out tracking (no external cameras needed) and supports Sense controllers for improved interactivity.

Controllers

Oculus Touch Controller

Designed for Meta Quest headsets, these controllers provide precise motion tracking, haptic feedback, and an ergonomic design. The latest versions (Touch Plus for Quest 3) are lighter, with better battery life and improve tracking technology.

Play Station VR2

These controllers feature adaptive triggers (like the PS5’s DualSense controller), haptic feedback, and finger touch detection for natural interactions. They support motion tracking via the PS VR2 headset’s cameras and enable realistic gameplay experiences.

Vive Focus Vision Controller

Vive Focus Vision Controller – The dedicated controller for HTC’s Vive Focus Vision headset. It offers six degrees of freedom (6DoF) tracking, an ergonomic design, and precise input detection, making it ideal for enterprise and consumer VR applications.

PC or Console

PlayStation 5 (PS5)

Sony’s flagship gaming console that powers PlayStation VR2. It features ray-tracing technology, fast load times with its SSD, and high-performance hardware (AMD Ryzen Zen 2 CPU & RDNA 2 GPU). It also has a strong VR game lineup, including titles like Resident Evil Village VR and Horizon Call of the Mountain.

Asus ROG Zephyrus G16

This gaming laptop features an AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 CPU and Nvidia RTX 4070 GPU, offering substantial power for VR gaming. Its sleek design and high-quality display make it suitable for both gaming and productivity tasks.

Lenovo Legion Go S

The Lenovo Legion Go S is a Windows-based handheld gaming PC equipped with an AMD Ryzen Z2 Go processor and an 8-inch variable refresh rate screen. While it offers portability, it faces challenges with Windows' interface for handheld gaming. Lenovo plans to release SteamOS versions later in 2025 to improve the user experience.

VR Game Trailers

These serve as an exciting look into virtual reality games. Trailers showcase immersive gameplay, stunning visuals, and unique mechanics designed to transport players into fully interactive digital worlds. Whether highlighting intense action, deep storytelling, or multiplayer experiences, VR game trailers build anticipation and give players a glimpse of what to expect in the evolving world of virtual reality gaming.

Batman: Arkham Shadow

Developed by Camouflaj and published by Oculus Studios, this game immerses players in the "Arkhamverse" as Batman faces a new adversary, the Rat King. Praised for its engaging gameplay mechanics, including stealth, detective work, and combat, it offers 10 to 15 hours of immersive experience.

Alien: Rogue Incursion

Set between the events of 'Alien' and 'Aliens', this survival horror game follows former Marine Zula Hendricks navigating a facility overrun by xenomorphs. Released for PS5 and Windows in December 2024, with a Meta Quest 3 version in February 2025, it captures the atmospheric tension of the Alien franchise.

Gorilla Tag

A deceptively simple yet highly engaging game where players embody gorillas playing tag. By June 2024, it amassed 10 million total users and 90,000 concurrent players, making it one of the most popular VR titles.

Behemoth

Developed by Skydance Interactive and released in late 2024, 'Behemoth' is a VR game that draws inspiration from 'Shadow of the Colossus'. Players take on the role of Wren, a hunter seeking a cure for a curse by battling gigantic creatures known as Behemoths. The game is noted for its impressive graphics and intense combat sequences.

Minecraft VR for Meta Quest

Developed by Mojang Studios, Minecraft VR for Meta Quest brings the iconic sandbox game into full virtual reality. Released as a standalone version, it allows players to explore, build, and survive in an immersive blocky world. With motion controls and a 360-degree view, the game enhances creativity and adventure like never before.

Among Us

The popular social deduction game has been adapted for VR, allowing players to experience tasks and impostor hunts in an immersive 3D environment.
